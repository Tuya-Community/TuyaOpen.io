# What is TuyaOpen?

**TuyaOpen** is an open source IoT development platform that provides SDKs, tools, and cloud services for building intelligent connected devices.

## What Can You Build With TuyaOpen?

- Smart home devices (lights, switches, sensors)
- AI-powered edge devices (voice control, AI vision)
- Industrial IoT solutions
- Custom embedded applications

## Supported Hardware

TuyaOpen supports a wide range of hardware:
- **ESP32** series (ESP32-S3, ESP32-C3)
- **Tuya T5 AI Board**
- **Arduino-compatible boards**
- And more...
