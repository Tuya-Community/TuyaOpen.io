The Tuya [AI Agent Dev Platform](https://platform.tuya.com/exp/ai) lets you build AI agents on top of large models and manage smart devices through them. You configure an agent on the platform, then publish it to apps and devices or integrate it into your own systems through APIs and SDKs.

## Why use the Tuya AI Agent Dev Platform

- **One-stop development platform** — the features and supporting capabilities you need are built in, ready to use without extra development.
- **Hallucination controls** — in IoT, AI hallucinations can cause serious problems. The platform addresses them with batch testing, workflows, and retrieval-augmented generation (RAG).
- **Things data model agent** — the agent talks to devices directly, so you skip the complex work of wiring a large model to device control.
- **Multi-model collaboration** — the platform pairs large models with Tuya's small models for faster responses, more efficient interaction, and lower compute cost on device control.
- **Open and extensible** — custom development is supported, so you can extend an agent's capabilities as your product needs grow.
